Timestamp Microservice

How to use this microservice:

Retrieve a unix and utc timestamp by entering a date or unix time in the address bar.

The correct format is [url]/api/yyyy-mm-dd or [url]/api/unixCodeGoesHere.

Examples:

  • [project url]/api/2015-12-25
  • [project url]/api/1451001600000
